Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you book a session or contact us, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. Payment information is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details.

How we use it

We use your information solely to confirm and manage your appointments, send you booking confirmations, and respond to your messages. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate this site:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Your payment data is governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

  • Resend — transactional email delivery for booking confirmations.

Data retention

We retain your booking information for up to 2 years for record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@thewellnessco.com.
