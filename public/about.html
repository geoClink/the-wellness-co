About

A practice built on presence, not pressure.

I came to this work the way many do — through my own unraveling. A decade ago I was running on empty, certain that healing meant pushing harder. It didn't. What changed everything was being met with stillness instead of solutions.

Since then I've trained across both energetic and clinical traditions, drawn to the places where they overlap. QHHT taught me to trust the wisdom of the subconscious. Acupuncture grounded me in the body's intelligent flow. Biofield tuning and Cognomovement showed me how gently the nervous system can be invited to let go.

Every session is unhurried and tailored to you. I hold no agenda for what you should feel or release — only a steady, attentive space where your own field can recalibrate.
